Xanla is a halfling rogue/warlock who carries a mysterious talking weapon.  She claims to be a member of the [[People/Black Saints/Black Saints\|Black Saints]] and tried to kill [[People/Player Characters/Cock\|Cock]] in order to take his weapon to add to her own power.  She was unsuccessful and died in the attempt.
